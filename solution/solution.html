    The noise from the air conditioning unit outside the dormitory:

1. Soundproof windows: Installing soundproof windows can effectively reduce the outdoor noise entering the dormitory.
2. Soundproof fence: Building a soundproof fence or wall around the air conditioning unit can reduce noise propagation.
3. Sound-absorbing materials: Using sound-absorbing materials such as plants or soundproof panels around outdoor walls or ground can reduce noise reflection and propagation.

    Environmental noise:

1. Earplugs or headphones: Use earplugs or noise-cancelling headphones to isolate external noise.
2. Improving indoor environment: Adding indoor plants or using sound-absorbing materials to reduce indoor noise.
3. Avoiding high noise periods: Try to avoid activities during the most severe environmental noise periods.

    Exercise noise:

1. Choosing low-noise exercise equipment: If you are using fitness equipment, place shock-absorbing pads or low-noise equipment underneath.
2. Establishing exercise time slots: Negotiate with roommates or neighbors to establish specific time slots for high-noise exercise to reduce interference with others.

    Other suggestions:

1. Reasonable layout: Reduce sound propagation through a reasonable indoor layout, such as placing furniture against walls to reduce sound reflection.
2. Equipment maintenance: Ensure the normal operation and maintenance of outdoor equipment to reduce unnecessary noise.
